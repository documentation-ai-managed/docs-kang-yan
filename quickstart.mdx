## Before You Begin

<Callout kind="info">
Before starting, ensure you have:
- A valid email address for account verification
- Access to `https://dashboard.example.com` (Kang Yan dashboard)
- A web browser like Chrome or Firefox
</Callout>

Kang Yan simplifies ordering meals from your favorite restaurants. Follow these steps to create an account, explore the dashboard, and place your first order.

## Create and Verify Your Account

Sign up takes under 2 minutes. Use the registration form at `https://dashboard.example.com/register`.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit Registration Page" icon="external-link">
    Open your browser and navigate to `https://dashboard.example.com/register`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in Details" icon="user">
    Enter your name, email, and a strong password. Agree to the terms.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit and Verify" icon="mail">
    Click **Register**. Check your email for a verification link from Kang Yan and click it to activate your account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or request a resend from the login page.
</Callout>

## Navigate the Dashboard

Log in at `https://dashboard.example.com/login`. The dashboard provides quick access to menus, past orders, and settings.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Menu Browser" icon="menu">
    Click **Browse Menus** on the left sidebar. Filter by cuisine, price, or rating. Use search for specific items like "pad thai".
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Order History" icon="history">
    View **My Orders** to track deliveries. Each order shows status: preparing, out for delivery, or completed.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Profile" icon="settings">
    Update your address and payment methods under **Account Settings**.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Place Your First Order

Select a restaurant and add items to your cart.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose Restaurant" icon="restaurant">
    From the menu browser, pick a restaurant like "Spicy Noodles Hub".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Items" icon="shopping-cart">
    Click **Add to Cart** on desired dishes. Customize quantities and notes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Checkout" icon="credit-card">
    Review cart, enter delivery address, select payment (card or wallet), and confirm.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track Order" icon="map-pin">
    Get a confirmation email and track live status in **My Orders**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

For developers, place orders via API:

<CodeGroup tabs="JavaScript,cURL">
  ```javascript
  const response = await fetch('https://api.example.com/v1/orders', {
    method: 'POST',
    headers: { 'Authorization': 'Bearer YOUR_TOKEN', 'Content-Type': 'application/json' },
    body: JSON.stringify({
      restaurantId: 'rest_123',
      items: [{ id: 'dish_456', quantity: 2 }],
      deliveryAddress: '123 Main St, City'
    })
  });
  const order = await response.json();
  console.log(order.id);
  ```
  ```bash
  curl -X POST https://api.example.com/v1/orders \
    -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_TOKEN" \
    -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
    -d '{
      "restaurantId": "rest_123",
      "items": [{"id": "dish_456", "quantity": 2}],
      "deliveryAddress": "123 Main St, City"
    }'
  ```
</CodeGroup>

<ParamField path="restaurantId" param-type="string" required="true">
  Unique restaurant identifier.
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="items" param-type="array" required="true">
  Array of item objects with `id` and `quantity`.
</ParamField>
